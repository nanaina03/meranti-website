Overview
Who We Are.

Meranti Interactive is an entertainment firm that specialises in designing video games. For this purpose, the firm provides game production services to designers all over the world. To capitalize on its technological prowess and creativity, the firm not only designs its own concepts but also collaborates with other firms all around the globe.

Our
Vision

To establish ourselves as a game company that will set standards in excellence in co-development and interactivity within the digital entertainment industry.

Our
Mission

To produce good games through collaboration with companies throughout the world in developing our gaming industry.

Core
Values

  • Creative Quality: Fun, entertaining, detailed designs.
  • Innovation: Using technology to make better games.
  • Teamwork: Collaborating to reach our goals smoothly.
  • Reliability: Consistent work and high-quality services.

Our Markets
Who We Build For.

Business Clients (B2B)

We assist foreign game developers in meeting complex deadlines and maintaining quality. Our co-development services directly assist in creating 3D models (characters, environments) and upgrading technical performance.

Game Players (B2C)

Simulation Gamers (18-35): Players who enjoy games where they can think in advance, plan, and manage resources.

RPG Gamers (16-40): Players who like character creation, discovering new places, and getting immersed in deep storylines and action.

HISTORY
Our History
The Journey So Far.
2021
Studio Founded
Meranti Interactive was established in Kuala Lumpur with a mission to elevate Southeast Asian game development to the global stage.
2024
Hardware Conceptualization
We began R&D on the M.A.P. Console to solve stick drift and provide gamers with a premium, zero-compromise handheld experience.
2025
Global Partnerships
Successfully executed AAA co-development projects, partnering with industry giants like Ubisoft (France) and Passion Republic Games.
2026
Award-Winning Expansion
Recognized as the Rising Game Development Studio (SEA) and expanded our team to support both proprietary IP and massive co-dev pipelines.
